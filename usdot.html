The Alignment

Shared Mission:
Zero Preventable Deaths

The U.S. Department of Transportation's National Roadway Safety Strategy calls for a Safe System approach — building redundancy into infrastructure so that human error does not result in death. Ætheris is purpose-built for exactly this.

Our sensor networks detect roadway departures automatically, alert approaching drivers before collisions occur, and dispatch emergency services the moment a crash happens — every layer of the Safe System addressed by one infrastructure deployment.

We are not a research project. We are a deployment-ready system seeking a federal partner to accelerate installation in the corridors that need it most.

What Ætheris Brings

Our Offer to USDOT

Deployment-ready technology that advances every pillar of the National Roadway Safety Strategy — built for rural corridors where federal investment is needed most.

Roadway Departure

Automatic Crash Detection & EMS Dispatch

WatchPost units detect vehicle departures and crashes within 200ms and fire automatic 911 CAD alerts with GPS coordinates — directly reducing the rural discovery delay that turns survivable crashes into fatalities.

Driver Warning

Real-Time Hazard Alerting

PathSign dynamic displays activate in under one second on confirmed detections — wildlife, stalled vehicles, debris, crash scenes. Trusted warnings that drivers respond to because they only fire on real events.

Infrastructure Data

Corridor Intelligence for Planning

Anonymized event data from every deployment feeds DOT planning systems — crash-risk mapping, seasonal hazard windows, infrastructure investment prioritization — building the evidence base for future safety decisions.

Technology

Privacy-First, FHWA-Compliant Architecture

No cameras. No license plate recognition. No facial recognition. The system is architecturally incapable of surveillance — designed from the ground up for federal procurement and community trust.

Connectivity

Off-Grid Rural Deployment Capability

Solar-powered, mesh-networked, satellite-uplink-capable. Ætheris operates in the corridors that lack cellular and grid infrastructure — precisely where USDOT's rural safety gap is widest.

Integration

ATMS & Open API Compatibility

CorridorLink bridges Ætheris networks to existing Traffic Management Systems using NTCIP-compatible protocols — no custom development on the agency side, ready for integration with current DOT infrastructure.

Strategic Alignment

How We Advance the
National Roadway Safety Strategy

🛣️

Safer Roads

Every Ætheris corridor deployment transforms passive infrastructure into an active safety layer — detecting hazards, warning drivers, and responding to crashes without human intervention.

🚘

Safer Vehicles

PathSign V2X broadcast modules transmit event data directly to AV-compatible vehicles — positioning Ætheris as critical infrastructure for the autonomous vehicle transition USDOT is overseeing.

🚑

Post-Crash Care

Automatic EMS dispatch with GPS precision addresses USDOT's post-crash care pillar directly — the seconds between crash and notification determine whether rural crashes are survivable.

🌐

Safer Speeds

Triggered warning signs that only activate on real detections achieve the speed reductions that static signage cannot — drivers respond because the alerts are credible, not ignored through habituation.

🔒

Privacy & Equity

No surveillance capability — by hardware design. Rural and tribal communities that bear the highest crash burden can trust that safety infrastructure will not become surveillance infrastructure.

📊

Data-Driven Planning

Corridor event data advances USDOT's evidence-based approach to infrastructure investment — building the national dataset needed to target safety dollars where they have the most impact.
