// 01 · Mission

Where Technology
Meets Conservation

Ætheris was founded to solve real problems at the intersection of public safety, environmental stewardship, and intelligent automation. Our flagship system — the Adaptive Wildlife Collision Mitigation and Prevention System (AWCMP), branded publicly as Sentinels of the Wild — addresses hundreds of human fatalities, thousands of injuries, and billions in damages caused by wildlife-vehicle collisions annually.

We operate under core tenets of evidence-based engineering, transparent capability disclosure, and privacy-preserving architecture — building systems that governments and communities can trust.

Adaptive Wildlife Collision
Mitigation & Prevention

AI-powered roadway wildlife detection — protecting lives, preserving ecosystems

The AWCMP system integrates distributed sensing networks, thermal imaging, edge computing, and centralized AI analytics to detect wildlife near roadways and deliver real-time alerts — all without surveillance capabilities. Designed for USDOT, FHWA, USFWS, and NSF federal grant pathways.

// 04 · Principles

What We
Stand On

01
Privacy by Design
Security and privacy are not features — they are foundational. Every system we build excludes surveillance capabilities from the architectural blueprint.
02
Transparent Limitations
We communicate honestly about what our systems can and cannot do. Evidence-based messaging over marketing hype, always.
03
Fail-Safe Engineering
In safety-critical deployments, failure modes matter as much as success cases. Our architectures are designed to degrade gracefully.
04
Ecological Sensitivity
Technology that serves conservation must not harm the ecosystems it protects. We design with biological and environmental context in mind.
05
Community Trust
Comprehensive stakeholder engagement spanning public, private, tribal, and community partners — because technology without trust cannot scale.
