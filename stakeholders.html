PRINCIPLE 01
🔄

One Infrastructure, Many Missions

AWCMP does not ask each stakeholder to fund a separate system. A single corridor deployment delivers value to all eight groups simultaneously — multiplying impact from every dollar invested.

PRINCIPLE 02
🤝

Partnership, Not Extraction

Every stakeholder relationship is designed to be genuinely mutual. We document what we bring and what we need in return — and we honor both sides of that exchange before asking for anything else.

PRINCIPLE 03
🔐

No Surveillance. Ever.

No stakeholder relationship — including law enforcement — gives AWCMP permission to surveil people. License plate recognition, facial recognition, and persistent human tracking are excluded in hardware. Not policy. Hardware.
